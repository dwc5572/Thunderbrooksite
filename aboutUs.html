About us


Thunderbrook Farm was founded in 1997 by Carolynn Walter Connolly.

Carolynn has been involved with riding and showing horses for over 40 years.

Carolynn teaches beginners through advanced riders starting at age 5.


Carolynn was named Trainer Of The Year of the Chester County Horse Show Association:
2009
2011
2019

Amenities include mats in stalls, tack room, hot and cold water, heated wash stall with mats, heated kitchen and outdoor sand ring with lights.
