Thunderbrook Farm


Thunderbrook Farm is conveniently located minutes from Rt. 422 in Collegeville, Pennsylvania.

We are a full service facility catering to the horse enthusiast striving to improve their riding skills.

We offer board, training and lessons in hunter and equitation.


Whether training at advanced levels, fine tuning your skills or simply riding for pleasure, you will always find a warm and friendly atmosphere at Thunderbrook Farm.
